Actor-Critic
Repeat:
Sample \( \{s_i, a_i\} \) using \( \pi_\theta(a|s) \)
Compute \( \hat{R}_i \, \forall \, i \)
\( \phi \gets \arg\min_\phi \frac{1}{2} \sum_i \| V_\phi(s_i) - \hat{R}_i \|^2 \)
\( \hat{A}_i \gets r(s_i, a_i) + \gamma V_\phi(s'_i) - V_\phi(s_i) \)
\( \theta \gets \theta + \alpha\big(\sum_i \nabla_\theta\log\pi_\theta(a_i|s_i) \hat{A}_i\big) \)
